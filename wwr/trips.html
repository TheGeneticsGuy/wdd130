Grand Canyon Trips

Group Adventures

Any adventure of 6 or more people and receive a discounted rate! This applies to one-day and multi-day adventures. Let us accomodate your group.

Half Day Raft Trip

This is our most popular excursion. Enjoy up to Grade 3 rapids that all ages can enjoy. Book early to reserve a spot as they will sell out early!

Full Day Raft Trip

Enjoy a combination of Grade 3 rapids, to scenic areas of the canyon that you can swim alongside the raft. Breakfast and lunch will be included.

Grade 5 Rafting - Full Day

Ready to experience the most extreme rafting in the canyon? This adventure is only for the most physically fit. GoPros will be provided for each adventurer.

3 Day Raft Trip

Rafting, camping, fishing, hiking, and all manner of outdoor adventure. All food will be provided, as well as an opportunity to learn survival skills.

The Ultimate 5 Day Trip

For the person who wants to do it all. Experience the longest route, with stops for camping, hikes to scenic lookouts, and fishing. Enjoy the adventure of a lifetime.
