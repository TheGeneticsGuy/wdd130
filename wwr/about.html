History

A group of friends obsessed with the outdoors, adventures, and creating experience loved river rafting and taking on challenges. These unforgettable experiences, documented for years, coalesced into a desire to want to help the world reconnect with our roots, to help people find the same joy and love for this planet that the team also has. Thus, Rapture was born as a way to share the opportunity and experiences for adventure as well.
