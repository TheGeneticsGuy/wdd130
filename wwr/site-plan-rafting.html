Overview

Purpose

Help people book, plan, and run white water rafting vacations that involve visiting remote wilderness areas that very few people get to see each year. Travel through national parks with scenic rivers, canyons, and amazing destinations. Travling through these amazing places on wild river rapids is an adventure that will never be forgotten and the goal of the website is to help sell these adventures to customers.

Audience

1) Who are the target cutomers? People of all ages who are seeking adventure. Since rapids can be of all skill levels, there are moderate rapids for the more amateur, younger, or elderly, and family friendly crowds.
2) What do they want? Adventure. Outdoors. Scenery. Excitement. Memories.
3) How will my audience usually access my site? Mobile device mostly, but I am sure when final booking is done it will still often be done on a desktop/laptop.
