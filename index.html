Arizona

Arizona is a state in the Southewest region of the USA. It shares a border with California on West, and Mexico on the bottom, New Mexico at the East, and Utah at the North. We are most known for The Grand Canyon, amzing outdoor camping and hiking destinations at our National Parks, as well as the famous Sonoran Desert featuring the tall Saguaro cacti. The state is also famous for being the sunniest state in the country, with close to an average of 300 sunny days per year. The capital of Arizona is the city of Phoenix.
